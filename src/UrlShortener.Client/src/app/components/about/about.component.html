How It Works

Our URL shortener service simplifies long, complex web addresses into brief, manageable links. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter Your URL: Paste your long URL into the input field above.
  2. Generate Short Link: We create a unique, short code for your URL using our advanced algorithm.
  3. Store and Create: We securely store the link pair and generate a new short URL.
  4. Share and Use: Copy your new short link and share it anywhere!
  5. Instant Redirection: When someone clicks your short link, they're seamlessly directed to your original URL.

Our Short URL Generation Algorithm

We use a robust and efficient algorithm to create short, unique URL paths:

  • The algorithm generates a random string of 6 characters by default.
  • It uses a mix of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, and numbers for maximum uniqueness.
  • This provides over 56 billion possible combinations, ensuring a low chance of collisions.
  • The length is customizable, allowing for even more possibilities if needed.

Creating the Final Short URL

Once the short path is generated, we combine it with our domain to create the final short URL:

  • We use a secure HTTPS protocol for all short URLs.
  • The domain is configurable, allowing for custom branded short links.
  • The final format is: https://[our-domain]/Url/nav/[short-path]

This approach ensures that our short URLs are:

  • Secure and trustworthy
  • Highly unique to avoid conflicts
  • Easy to read and share
  • Customizable for branding purposes
